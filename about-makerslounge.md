# MakersLounge

## Our Story
Passionate people can change the world. We're here to enable them through community. Whether you're a first-time creator or a seasoned builder, you're welcome here.

## Why MakersLounge Exists
Started by Berto because finding the right people to build with is hard. You have an idea, maybe even a prototype, but finding a collaborator who complements your skills? That's the real challenge. No gatekeeping, no cliques — just a welcoming space where half-baked ideas are celebrated and the right connections happen naturally.

## What We Do
- **MakersLounge Podcast** — Candid conversations with founders sharing their stories
- **Events at New Stadium** — In-person gatherings in Toronto where makers meet and connect
- **AI Workshops** — Hands-on sessions to help makers leverage AI tools
- **AI Consulting** — Strategic guidance for businesses integrating AI

## Founders
- **Berto Mill** — Multi-disciplinary builder (code, design, business). Started MakersLounge after struggling to find collaborators.
- **Katy Rozanova** — Passionate about building community and connecting makers with the right people.

## Values
- **Everyone belongs here** — Experience level doesn't matter, building something does
- **No gatekeeping** — If you're curious and want to create, you're one of us
- **Share early, share often** — Half-baked ideas deserve to see the light
- **Real connections over networking** — Finding people you genuinely want to build with
