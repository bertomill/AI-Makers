About Event
​AI Makers - Build AI tools together on a Monday evening.

​On Monday March 2, MakersLounge hosts a hands-on working session where we get into the code, see real workflows from experienced professionals who use AI tools every day, and walk you through building your own workflow from scratch.

​No lectures. No slides. Just building.

​What to expect: We'll tackle a project and build it out in real time. Think AI agents, automations, chatbots, workflows. By the end of the session, you'll walk away with a real, working tool or project you can actually use in your day to day.

​Who this is for:

​Developers curious about AI agents and automation

​Founders building AI into their products

​Makers who learn best by doing

​No experience required. Just bring a laptop and a willingness to get your hands dirty.
